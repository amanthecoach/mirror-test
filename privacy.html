Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our products:

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver your Mirror Test report to your email

3. Your Audit Answers

Your answers to the 12 Mirror Test questions are transmitted to Anthropic's API solely for the purpose of generating your personal report. They are not stored on our servers, are not read by us, and are permanently discarded once your report is generated. We have no access to your individual answers after the report is created.

By purchasing the 30-Day Mirror Protocol, you consent to receiving emails over 30 days as part of the programme. You may contact us at any time to stop the sequence.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except the following services which are necessary to deliver our products:

6. Data Retention

Your email address is retained for the duration of the 30-Day Protocol and for a reasonable period thereafter for support purposes. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All payment processing is handled by Razorpay using industry-standard encryption.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time.
